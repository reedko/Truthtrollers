Founder story

TruthTrollers

TruthTrollers is my answer to one of the hardest problems on the internet: how people can investigate claims together instead of just arguing past one another. The platform structures evidence, sources, claims, ratings, and reasoning so online content can be examined rather than merely reacted to.

AI-powered evidence workflowA system that extracts claims, organizes supporting and refuting material, and helps people analyze what they are seeing.
Founder-built from the ground upArchitecture, backend, frontend, browser extension logic, infrastructure, workflow design, and product iteration.
Big mission, practical executionThe aim is ambitious. The build is concrete: one module, one system, one useful tool at a time.
