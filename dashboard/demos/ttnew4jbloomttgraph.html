Notice that Source B emits one source claim that nuances Claim 1 and another that supports Claim 2. That one-to-many behavior is a good graph smell.
In a real product, node labels could truncate in the graph and expand in a details panel on click, just like Neo4j Bloom keeps the canvas tidy while preserving depth.
